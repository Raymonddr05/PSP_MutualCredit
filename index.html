PSP Mutual Credit

Building a global mutual credit network to transcend the current money system is the most important socio-techno-economic problem of our time.

The paper is available at:
https://provensuccess.org/PSP_Whitepaper_DelaRama_2026.pdf

The main properties:

  • Credit is created when goods or services are exchanged, and destroyed once spent back.
  • No bank or central authority is needed, and no interest is ever charged.
  • A unit of credit is anchored to a fixed basket of physical goods, protecting its value from both currency devaluation and crypto-style speculation.
  • Circulating credit always matches the goods and services available, preventing inflation.

Why are we building this?

Because in the current money system, no currency holds the same purchasing power over time.

We are building a network where what you earn holds its purchasing power independently of monetary policy, market confidence, or speculative demand.
